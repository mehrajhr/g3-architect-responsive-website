Brand New Group of Architects

G3 Architect has been providing architectural services in Bangladesh for over 26 years. The company was founded by Bayejid Mahbub Khondoker, who is now serving as the principal architect of Nakshabid. What’s interesting is that Nakshabid is the creative mind that designed the Bangabandhu Military Museum.
